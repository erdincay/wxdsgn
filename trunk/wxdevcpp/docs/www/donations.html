Support wxWidgets

If you use wx-DevCpp and find it to be useful in your work, I request you to donate couple of bucks to the wxWidgets development community. Your contribution to wxWidgets project will directly contribute to the quality and future bug fixes for wx-Devcpp

wxWidgets need lot of funding to develop further, so feel free to send your donation to Julian Smart. Please note even a small micropayment via paypal will be a great help.

Check wxWidgets donation page at http://wxwidgets.org/spi.htm
If possible, donate directly to wx-widgets otherwise, use the Donation link given in sourceforge.
